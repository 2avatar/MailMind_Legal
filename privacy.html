4. AI Processing & Third-Party Data Sharing

Important: When you use MailMind's AI features, email content (subject lines, sender names, and body excerpts up to 2,000 characters) is sent to Anthropic for processing via their Claude AI API.

About Anthropic (our AI sub-processor)

  • What is sent: Email metadata and body excerpts necessary to execute your command.
  • What is NOT sent: Attachments, full email threads, or data from emails you haven't asked about.
  • Training: Anthropic does not use data submitted via their commercial API to train AI models. This is stated in their commercial terms.
  • Retention: Anthropic retains API logs for up to 7 days for safety monitoring, then auto-deletes them.
  • DPA: Anthropic's Data Processing Agreement is automatically incorporated into their commercial terms of service and includes EU Standard Contractual Clauses for international transfers.
  • Opt-out: You may disable AI features in Settings at any time. MailMind's non-AI features (manual labeling, rules) will continue to work.

5. Automated Decision-Making (GDPR Art. 22)

MailMind's auto-pilot rules process your emails automatically every 15 minutes. These rules can label, archive, or categorize emails without manual intervention.

  • You control it: You create, enable, disable, and delete rules. No rules exist unless you explicitly create them.
  • Audit trail: All automated actions are logged. You can view what each rule did in the Activity Log panel.
  • Undo: Automated actions can be reversed via the Undo button.
  • Human intervention: You can pause all auto-pilot rules at any time from the Rules panel.
  • Right to contest: If an automated action produces an undesired result, you can undo it, disable the rule, or contact us.

6. Gmail API — Limited Use Disclosure

MailMind's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:

  • We only request the gmail.modify scope — the minimum required to read, label, archive, and send emails.
  • We do not use Gmail data for advertising, market research, or any purpose unrelated to providing the MailMind service to you.
  • We do not transfer Gmail data to third parties except as described in Section 4 (Anthropic for AI processing, with your consent).
  • Gmail data is not used to train generalist or foundational AI models.

7. Data Retention

  • OAuth tokens: Stored while your session is active. Revoked and deleted on sign-out.
  • Settings & rules: Stored until you delete them or delete your account.
  • Conversation history: In-memory only during your active session. Never written to disk. Lost on session end or server restart.
  • Server logs: Rotated at 5 MB. Email addresses are redacted in logs. Logs contain no email body content.
  • Anthropic: Retains API logs for up to 7 days, then auto-deletes.

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR (EU/EEA residents)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data (Art. 15) — use the "Export my data" feature in Settings.
  • Rectify inaccurate data (Art. 16) — edit your settings at any time.
  • Erase your data (Art. 17) — use "Delete all my data" in Settings, which permanently removes all your data from our servers, revokes all tokens, and signs out all devices.
  • Restrict processing (Art. 18) — disable individual features or pause auto-pilot rules.
  • Data portability (Art. 20) — export your data as JSON.
  • Object to processing (Art. 21) — contact us at the email above.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (Art. 7(3)) — sign out, disable AI features, or delete your account.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

Under CCPA/CPRA (California residents)

You have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect, use, and share.
  • Delete your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information — we do not sell or share your data.
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights.

To exercise any right, use the in-app settings or email mailaimind@gmail.com.

9. Data Security

  • OAuth tokens are stored server-side and never exposed to the frontend.
  • Session IDs use one-time authorization codes — never appear in URLs or browser history.
  • Sessions are bound to the browser fingerprint (User-Agent) to prevent hijacking.
  • All API endpoints are rate-limited.
  • CORS is restricted to the MailMind frontend origin only.
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) and other security headers are applied to all responses.
  • Prompt injection defenses protect against malicious email content influencing AI behavior.

10. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States by our sub-processors (Anthropic, Google, Stripe). These transfers are protected by EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) included in each sub-processor's data processing agreement.

11. Children

MailMind is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
